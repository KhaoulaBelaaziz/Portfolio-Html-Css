Hii there

In the academic year of 2021/2022, a fervent advocate for varied societal and technical causes emerged amidst the bustling life of the AIAC. The President of the AIAC Great Debaters demonstrated not merely a passion for dialectic engagements but a dedicated spirit towards imbuing a similar ardor among the new entrants of AIAC. With a meticulous eye for organizing and moderating debates across French, Arabic, and English languages, this individual curated platforms to delve into an array of themes and issues, thereby fostering a multicultural and multilingual debating environment.

Leadership permeated through tasks, leading a team assiduously in event planning and logistics, and ingeniously amplifying club membership by a staggering 30% through adept promotional and recruitment strategies. The eclectic interests didn’t merely pause at debates. A collaborative spirit was demonstrated as a member of the AIAC Electronics Club, involving in intricate electronics projects, focusing particularly on circuit design and prototyping. Humanitarian and altruistic endeavors were also in the spotlight, marked by an active participation in the Paradisiers Club, organizing a humanitarian caravan with a focus on a pivotal project: renovating a school, ensuring its successful completion through collaborative volunteer efforts. Furthermore, as a board member of the AIAC Animal Aid Club, contributions were made tangible in planning and executing initiatives aimed at animal welfare, thereby weaving a tapestry of technical, humanitarian, and environmental efforts throughout the academic year.
